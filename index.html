Introduction

Welcome to DrexLyfe, a game simulating the Freshman experience at Drexel University! Escape the drudgeries of your own life and live out all your fantasies, all while entertaining yourself with quips about life as a Drexel student. Manage your time and stats wisely though, or else you might just have to drop out (game over)! Get it now? Then try navigating through the menus and doing some of the many activities available to you.

Drexel's Motto is "Ambition Can't Wait", so now the question is: where will your ambition take you?
